# Standard Operating Procedures (SOP)

## Overview

This document provides standard operating procedures for common ERP business activities. These procedures are intended to guide users through standard business operations while ensuring compliance with organizational workflows.

---

# SOP 1: Creating a Sales Order

## Purpose

Create a customer sales order for products or services.

## Prerequisites

- Customer exists in the ERP system.
- Products are available.
- User has Sales module access.

## Procedure

1. Open the Sales module.
2. Navigate to Sales Orders.
3. Click **New Sales Order**.
4. Select the customer.
5. Add products or services.
6. Verify quantities and prices.
7. Save the order.
8. Submit for approval if required.
9. Confirm the order.

## Expected Result

A Sales Order is successfully created and assigned a unique order number.

## Common Mistakes

- Selecting the wrong customer
- Incorrect pricing
- Missing shipping address
- Invalid quantity

---

# SOP 2: Creating a Purchase Order

## Purpose

Purchase goods or services from a vendor.

## Prerequisites

- Vendor exists.
- Required approvals obtained.

## Procedure

1. Open Procurement module.
2. Navigate to Purchase Orders.
3. Click **New Purchase Order**.
4. Select vendor.
5. Add required items.
6. Review pricing.
7. Save the purchase order.
8. Submit for approval.
9. Send the purchase order to the vendor.

## Expected Result

Purchase Order is approved and ready for vendor processing.

## Common Mistakes

- Wrong vendor selection
- Duplicate purchase order
- Missing approval
- Incorrect delivery date

---

# SOP 3: Receiving Goods

## Purpose

Record receipt of purchased goods.

## Procedure

1. Open Inventory module.
2. Select Goods Receipt.
3. Reference the Purchase Order.
4. Verify delivered quantity.
5. Inspect goods.
6. Record accepted quantities.
7. Save the transaction.

## Expected Result

Inventory is updated successfully.

## Common Mistakes

- Incorrect quantity entered
- Wrong warehouse selected
- Damaged items accepted without inspection

---

# SOP 4: Creating a Customer Invoice

## Purpose

Generate an invoice after goods or services are delivered.

## Procedure

1. Open Finance or Sales module.
2. Locate the completed Sales Order.
3. Create Invoice.
4. Verify customer details.
5. Verify tax calculations.
6. Post the invoice.

## Expected Result

Invoice is generated and available for customer payment.

## Common Mistakes

- Incorrect tax code
- Missing customer information
- Duplicate invoice generation

---

# SOP 5: Vendor Payment

## Purpose

Process payment to suppliers.

## Procedure

1. Open Finance module.
2. Navigate to Vendor Payments.
3. Select approved invoice.
4. Verify payment amount.
5. Select payment method.
6. Process payment.
7. Record transaction.

## Expected Result

Vendor payment is successfully recorded.

## Common Mistakes

- Paying the wrong invoice
- Duplicate payment
- Incorrect bank account
- Payment without approval

---

# General SOP Guidelines

- Always verify master data before starting a transaction.
- Follow approval workflows.
- Record transactions immediately after completion.
- Report unexpected system errors to the ERP support team.
- Never bypass mandatory approval steps.

---

# Guidance Notice

These procedures provide functional guidance only. Users must not modify ERP configurations or attempt actions beyond their assigned permissions.
