# ERP Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

## Overview

This document contains frequently asked questions related to ERP business processes, transactions, user roles, approvals, and common functional concepts.

---

## Q1. What is an ERP system?

An ERP (Enterprise Resource Planning) system integrates business processes such as Sales, Purchasing, Inventory, Finance, Human Resources, and Manufacturing into a single platform.

---

## Q2. What is the purpose of a Sales Order?

A Sales Order records a customer's request to purchase products or services before fulfillment and invoicing.

---

## Q3. What is a Purchase Order?

A Purchase Order (PO) is an official document issued to a vendor requesting goods or services.

---

## Q4. What is a Goods Receipt?

A Goods Receipt records that purchased goods have been received and updates inventory.

---

## Q5. What is a Customer Invoice?

A Customer Invoice requests payment from a customer after products or services have been delivered.

---

## Q6. What is Vendor Payment?

Vendor Payment records payment made to a supplier for approved invoices.

---

## Q7. What is Master Data?

Master Data includes relatively stable business information such as:

- Customers
- Vendors
- Products
- Warehouses
- Employees
- Cost Centers

---

## Q8. What is Transaction Data?

Transaction Data is created during daily business operations, such as Sales Orders, Purchase Orders, Goods Receipts, and Invoices.

---

## Q9. Why are approvals required?

Approvals ensure that transactions comply with organizational policies and financial controls.

---

## Q10. What should I do if a transaction is awaiting approval?

Wait for the designated approver or contact the responsible approver. Do not bypass the approval workflow.

---

## Q11. What is the Order-to-Cash process?

Order-to-Cash manages the complete customer sales cycle, from Sales Order creation through payment collection.

---

## Q12. What is the Procure-to-Pay process?

Procure-to-Pay manages purchasing activities from Purchase Requisition to Vendor Payment.

---

## Q13. What is Record-to-Report?

Record-to-Report manages financial recording, reconciliation, period closing, and reporting.

---

## Q14. Why is my transaction rejected?

Common reasons include:

- Missing mandatory fields
- Invalid master data
- Approval pending
- Closed accounting period
- User permission issues

---

## Q15. What should I do if I receive "Permission Denied"?

Contact your ERP Administrator to verify your assigned security role.

---

## Q16. Can this assistant execute ERP transactions?

No.

This assistant provides functional guidance only.

---

## Q17. Can this assistant modify ERP configuration?

No.

Configuration changes must be performed by authorized ERP administrators.

---

## Q18. Can this assistant access live ERP data?

No.

The assistant does not have access to live ERP environments or organizational data.

---

## Q19. When should I contact ERP Support?

Contact ERP Support when:

- You encounter system errors.
- Your account lacks required permissions.
- Configuration changes are required.
- The issue cannot be resolved using documented procedures.

---

## Q20. What information should I provide when asking for help?

To receive accurate guidance, provide:

- ERP Module
- Your role
- Transaction name
- Error message (if any)
- Brief description of the issue

---
